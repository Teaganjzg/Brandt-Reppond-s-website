Hi there, I'm Brandt

My name is Brandt Reppond and I'm an audio engineer working in Denver, Colorado. I've been recording for two years on my own and have been interning with the very professional staff at Third and James Studios since June of 2018. Audio is my passion and my goal is to help other musicians share there creativity with the world.
